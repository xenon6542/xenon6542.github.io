    • 精神品质类作文-举例论证（袁隆平，坚持）
    2021-05-25 / 2 min read

    The following example, personally I think, could account for the importance of perseverance. In 1959 China, vast population was ravaged by food shortages. In attempting to solve the Great China Famine, Yuan Longping turned his mind to rice. However, it had been previously thought that heterosis was not possible for self-pollinating crops such as rice. Owing to his perseverance, he made his seminal discovery of the genetic basis of heterosis in rice in the 1960s. His perseverance to overcome the difficulty makes him become "the father of hybrid rice".（85 words）

    译文：我个人认为，下面这个例子可以解释坚持的重要性。1959年，中国大量人口遭受粮食短缺的蹂躏。袁隆平在试图解决中国大饥荒的过程中，将注意力转向了水稻，然而以前人们认为水稻等自花授粉作物不可能有杂种优势。由于他的坚持不懈，他在20世纪60年代发现了水稻杂种优势的遗传基础。他克服重重困难的毅力，使他成为「杂交水稻之父」。

    perseverance坚持不懈 可换成其他精神品质。
