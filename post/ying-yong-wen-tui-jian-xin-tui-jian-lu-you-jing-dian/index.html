应用文 推荐信 推荐旅游景点

2020-09-23

2017考研英语一 介绍北京 仿写介绍清远

2017年 推荐旅游景点 中国文化

关键词：

  • tourist attraction/ scenic spot 旅游景点
  • visit/ explore 游览
  • feature/ boast 有……特点的
  • well-known/ renowned/ famous/ celebrated 闻名遐迩的

表达与搭配：

  1. offers a quick glimpse into 短暂体味
  2. showcase 陈列
  3. antiquity 古迹, 古物
  4. a huge collection of = a vast array of =a magnificient array of 琳琅满目的
  5. extensive history 悠久的历史
  6. sophisticated culture 博大精深的文化
  7. spectacular view 壮丽的景观
  8. rolling mountains 绵延不绝的山脉
  9. recommend doing sth 建议
  10. make… worthwhile 使……非常值得
  11. tuck away sth 将……藏起来
  12. beacon tower 烽火台
  13. the Palace Museum 故宫
  14. the Great Wall 长城
  15. quaint 古色古香的 the quaint downtown captital 首都老城中心区

句式：

  • for表原因，不能放句首，要在句后

##范文：

Dear Prof. Cook,

​ Welcome to China! Knowing that you love travel, I'd like to recommend some of the most celerated tourist attractions in Beijing.

​ Firstly, I recommend exploring the Palace Museum, the world's largest ancient palace complex, because it showcases a huge collection of arts and antiquities which offers a quick glimpse into China's extensive history and sophisticated culture. Moreover, if you are interested in outdoor activities, I recommend visiting the Great Wall, for the spectacular view of rolling mountains atop the ancient beacon towers will definitely make your trip worthwhile. Besides, there are unique cafes and galleries tucked away in the old lanes and alleys, or Hutongs in Beijing. Riding a bike along the quaint downtown captital after a whole-day teaching is such an excellent way to experience the charming Hutong culture.

​ Hopefully my recommendations are helpful and wish you a delightful journey.

Yours sincerely,

Li Ming

##仿写：

伍教授要来你的家乡清远参观，希望你向他推荐一些清远的旅游景点。

关键词：

  • Guangdong Province 广东省
  • the Pearl River Delta 珠江三角洲
  • the Bei River 北江
  • the Lianzhou Subterranean River 连州地下河
  • stalactite 钟乳石
  • the Huangteng Canyon 黄腾峡
  • Qingyuan field broiler 清远麻鸡（ Beijing roast duck 北京烤鸭）
  • drifting 漂流

自己写：

Dear Prof. Wu,

​ At your request, I'd like to recommend some celebrated scenic beauties in my hometown to you.

​ Firstly, I highly recommend exploring the Lianzhou Subterranean River（连州地下河）, because it showcases a magnificent array of stalactites（钟乳石）which offers a quick glimpse into China's mysterious caves（洞穴）. Moreover, if you are interested in outdoor activities, I recommend drifting in the Huangteng Canyon, for the spectacular view of remarkable valleys will definitely make your trip worthwhile. Besides, rafting in the Bei River, there are unique restaurants tucked away in the boats, in which you can enjoy the cuisine, such as the Qingyuan field broiler and fresh fishes, to experience the charming Qingyuan culture.

Hopefully my recommendations are helpful and wish you a delightful journey.

Yours sincerely,

Li Ming

中文翻译：

尊敬的伍教授:

应你的要求，我想向你推荐一些我家乡的名胜古迹。

首先，我强烈推荐探索连州地下河，因为它展示了一系列壮观的钟乳石，让我们可以快速一瞥中国神秘的洞穴。此外，如果你对户外活动感兴趣，我推荐你在黄腾峡漂流，因为壮观的山谷景色绝对会让你的旅行值得一游。此外，在北江漂流，还有独特的餐馆藏在船上，在那里你可以享受美食，如清远麻鸡和鲜鱼，体验迷人的清远文化。

希望我的建议对你有所帮助，并祝你旅途愉快。

您真诚的,

李明
