    • CET-6 翻译练习题 老龄化问题
    2021-05-20 / 2 min read

    【原文】
    如今，中国正步入老龄化社会，因此独生子女一代面临着巨大的工作和生活压力。中国政府开始适当调整计划生育政策，允许一些家庭在特殊情况下生育二胎。但调查显示，很多夫妻迫于不断加重的经济压力，放弃生育二胎。因此，要从根本上解决老龄化的问题不能依靠出生率的上升，最有效的办法是建立有效的社会保障制度。

    【翻译词汇】

    1. 步入 step into

    2. 老龄化社会 aging society

    3. 独生子女一代 the only-child generation

    4. 巨大的 enormous

    5. 调整 adjust

    6. 计划生育政策 the family planning policy

    7. 在特殊情况下 under certain circumstances

    8. 由于，迫于 due to

    9. 放弃 abandon 依靠 rely on

    10. 出生率 birth rate

    11. 有效的 effective

    12. 社会保障制度 social security system

    13. economic pressure ≈ financial burden 经济压力

    14. 从根源上去解决问题: tackle the root of problems

    【译文为个人练习，非范文】

    Nowadays, China is stepping into the aging society, making the only-child generation face enormous pressure both from work and life. The Chinese government has begun to adjust the family planning policy, which allows some families to have a second child under particular circumstances. However, the survey indicates that many couples abandon this chance, owing to the increasing economic pressure. Therefore, in order to tackle the roots of the aging problem, counting on the rise in birth rate doesn't work and the best solution is to establish an effective social security system.
